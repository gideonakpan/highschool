Admissions

ADMISSIONS POLICY

Admission into Beulah International School is through a competitive entrance examination. Prospective students in JS1, JS2 & SS1 are admitted in September every year. Transfer students may be admitted anytime within the school year provided there is space in the required class. Parents are required to pay a non-refundable deposit of fifty thousand naira (N50, 000) which forms part of the school fees.

Age Requirements:

Prospective JS1 students should not be less than 10 years old by October of the year of admission.

SCHOOL FEES POLICY

Fees are paid directly into the schools account using a teller designated for this purpose. Receipts are issued out by the school upon the deposit of the teller. Cash and personal cheques are not accepted in the school.

Discount:

Parents with 2 children will be given a 5% discount on tuition fees, while those with 3 or more children will be given a 10% discount. All biological children of BIS staff will enjoy 30% discount on tuition fees. Discounts could also be given based on special conditions.

Books:

JSS1 students will pay for text and exercise books as part of the first term fees.

From JSS2 and above, texts and exercise books will be part of the 3rd term fees. This is to enable the school place order early enough from publishers. Students whose siblings have the recommended textbooks could be exempted.
