Academics

Tower of Ivory Schools run the Junior Secondary School and Senior Secondary School Programmes as specified in the Nigerian National Policy on Education. It offers a wide variety of academic subjects taught by a dedicated and professional staff. The student begins with a broad spectrum of subjects, which becomes more focused as he or she progresses.

Junior Secondary School

In the Junior Secondary School (Basic 7 to 9), the College emphasises the basic skills in the following subjects:.

  1. English Studies
  2. Mathematics
  3. Music
  4. Fine Art
  5. Efik/Ibibio Language
  6. Igbo Language
  7. French Language
  8. Business studies
  9. Basic Science
  10. Basic Technology
  11. Physical and Health Education
  12. Computer Studies
  13. Christian Religious Studies
  14. Social Studies
  15. Civic Education
  16. Security Education
  17. Home Economics
  18. Agricultural Science
