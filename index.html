Who we are

Sacred Heart School is a Catholic community which
provides an excellent academic education in an
environment of faith and virtue.

Sacred Heart School builds active partnership between school,
parish and family. Sacred Heart School graduates leaders who
are exceptionally well prepared to meet the challenges of college
preparatory education with a commitment to Catholic service to
others. We are accredited through the Western Catholic
Education Association
